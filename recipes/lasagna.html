Lasagna

The lasagna in Brazil is kinda different than some other countries

The brazillians eat lasagna with some rice and sometimes they put ketchup in the top

List of ingredients

  • 1 lasagna pasta (ready made)
  • 500 g of ham
  • 500 g of mozzarella cheese
  • 500 g minced meat
  • 1 prepared tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • oregano

    Step to step to make a good lasagna for your family

    1. Cook the lasagna pasta in approximately 2 liters of water for 5 minutes;
    2. In a pan, cook the ground beef, after cooking put tomato paste, salt and some spices in you want;
    3. Start by putting together a layer of sauce, lasagna noodles, ham and chesse;
    4. Do this process until everything is finished;
    5. Heat the oven ate 180Â°C for 5 minutes;
    6. Place the lasagna in the oven for 20 to 30 minutes
